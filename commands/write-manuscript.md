# Write Manuscript

Invoke the `manuscript-writing` skill to draft a medical research paper.

## Prerequisites (block if missing)

- `research-question.md` — clear PICO and hypothesis
- `results-summary.md` — completed analysis results
- Figures and tables — generated by `figure-generation`

If any prerequisite is missing, invoke the corresponding skill first.

## Writing order (NOT the paper order)

1. **Methods** — most objective, write first
2. **Results** — based on completed analysis
3. **Introduction** — now you know exactly what gap you fill
4. **Discussion** — requires most thought
5. **Abstract** — summarize the finished paper
6. **Title** — distill to one sentence

## Key rules

- Results: data only, no interpretation. "The AUC was 0.87" not "The excellent AUC of 0.87 demonstrates..."
- Discussion: never introduce new data not in Results
- Introduction: funnel shape — broad context → specific gap → your aim
- Tense: Methods/Results in past tense; Introduction/Discussion present tense for established knowledge
