# Research Ethics

Invoke the `research-ethics` skill to check ethical compliance.

**Note: This is a reminder, not a mandatory block. User is responsible for obtaining actual approvals.**

## Checklist

1. **IRB / Ethics Committee** — approval number, scope confirmation
2. **IACUC** (animal studies) — 3R principles, humane endpoints
3. **Informed Consent** — prospective: written consent / retrospective: waiver with justification
4. **Data Privacy** — de-identification, GDPR/HIPAA/个人信息保护法 compliance
5. **Trial Registration** — ClinicalTrials.gov / ChiCTR / PROSPERO (before enrollment)
6. **COI & Funding** — conflict of interest + funding source declarations

## Output
Ethics statement template for Methods section.

## Methods Template
```
This study was approved by [Institution] Ethics Committee (No. XXX),
in accordance with the Declaration of Helsinki.
[Written informed consent was obtained / Informed consent was waived due to ...]
```
