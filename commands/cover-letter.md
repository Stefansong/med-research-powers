# Cover Letter Writing

Invoke the `cover-letter-writing` skill to draft a cover letter for journal submission.

## Prerequisites
- Completed manuscript (`manuscript-writing`)
- Confirmed target journal (`journal-selection`)

## Workflow

### 4-Paragraph Structure (≤1 page)
1. **Hook** — why this finding matters (NOT "Dear Editor, we are pleased to submit...")
2. **Novelty** — specific comparison with 2-3 existing studies
3. **Journal Fit** — cite 1-2 recent articles from target journal, explain why their readers need this
4. **Declarations** — not under review elsewhere, all authors approved, COI, trial registration

### Journal Style Adaptation
- Nature/Science/Cell → broad impact, cross-disciplinary
- Lancet/JAMA/BMJ → clinical relevance, patient impact
- Specialty journals → domain gap, technical contribution

### Cascade Rewrite (if rejected)
When resubmitting to a different journal: rewrite Paragraph 3 (Journal Fit), adjust Paragraph 1 hook angle, update cited journal articles.

## Output
`cover-letter.md` — ready for submission.
